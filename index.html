The Opportunity

THE MARKET IS MOVING
TOWARD ZERO

Gen Z is drinking less than any generation before it. Alcohol consumption has declined for four consecutive years. The sober-curious movement is not a niche — it's an accelerating mainstream preference that no premium brand currently owns.

📉
Declining Alcohol Consumption
Per-capita alcohol consumption has declined for four consecutive years. Gen Z drinks roughly 20% less than Millennials did at the same age, driven by health consciousness and a shift toward functional beverages.
⚡
Functional Beverage Explosion
Consumers increasingly expect their beverages to do something — hydrate, energize, recover. Celsius grew revenue over 100% in 2023 on exactly this thesis. Sun Cruiser Zero enters this space with a social-occasion advantage Celsius cannot match.
🎯
White Space Nobody Owns
No brand currently sits at the intersection of social occasion credibility + functional energy + zero alcohol. Water and soda lack credibility. Celsius lacks social legitimacy at parties. Sun Cruiser Zero fills the gap.

01 · CORE CUSTOMER VALUE
Social Confidence Without Compromise
The job-to-be-done isn't "drink a tea" — it's "belong at the party, fully present, with zero pressure to drink alcohol." Sustained energy and hydration that fits seamlessly into an active lifestyle without the sugar crash or guilt of traditional energy drinks.
02 · ACTUAL PRODUCT
The Physical Can
Non-alc brewed iced tea with 200mg caffeine (green tea + guarana), 400mg sodium, 100mg potassium, 60mg magnesium, 2mg B6, 3mcg B12. Zero carbonation — bubble-free and smooth. The same Sun Cruiser flavors the consumer already loves.
03 · AUGMENTED PRODUCT
Brand Equity & Social Identity
Something you can drink at the gym or a party that implicitly promises no hangover, no sugar crash, and no compromise — with the social currency of something that looks and feels like a real drink. MLB, NFL, and AEG concert series partnerships transfer directly.
04 · DESIRED PRODUCT
The Ideal the Consumer Actually Wants
A functional beverage for health-forward consumers who seek a drink that signals wellness, taste, and intention without compromising on flavor or energy performance. All the social ease of a drink — zero alcohol, zero regrets, full energy.
Competitive Positioning

CELSIUS VS.
SUN CRUISER ZERO

Sun Cruiser Zero matches Celsius on every functional credential that matters while winning on hydration, format, and social versatility — making it the stronger choice for health-conscious consumers who don't stop at the gym.

Points of Difference
Where We Win
  • Three-electrolyte hydration formula (400mg Na, 100mg K, 60mg Mg) — Celsius has none
  • Bubble-free format — lighter on the stomach, easier for all-day drinking
  • Social credibility of a lifestyle brand vs. a fitness brand — fits parties AND gyms
  • Sun Cruiser flavor DNA — familiar, premium, no artificial aftertaste
  • 0% ABV opens retail channels unavailable to alcoholic SKUs (gyms, campus stores, Amazon DTC)
  • No federal excise tax — stronger unit margins than all alcoholic Boston Beer products
Points of Parity
Where We Match
  • 200mg natural caffeine — equivalent to Celsius MetaPlus formula
  • 10 calories per can — near-zero calorie profile
  • 1g sugar — matches Celsius' low-sugar positioning
  • B vitamins (B6 + B12) — functional standard in the category
  • Natural flavor positioning — no artificial additives
  • $2.29/can retail price — 15% premium over Celsius NBA ($1.67), justified by social utility + brand appeal
Points of Contention
Where We Differ
  • Celsius claims thermogenesis (fat oxidation) — we do not; we lead on hydration instead
  • Celsius has deeper gym/fitness brand recognition — we have superior social occasion fit
  • Celsius is carbonated — we argue non-carbonated is a feature, not a limitation
  • Celsius has broader current distribution — we leverage Boston Beer's 50-state wholesale network
Resonating Focus Value Proposition
Sun Cruiser Zero enables sober-curious Gen Z college students (18–24) to participate fully in social occasions — delivering 200mg caffeine, a three-electrolyte hydration formula, and the same Sun Cruiser flavor they already trust — so they never have to choose between showing up and feeling great the next morning, unlike Celsius or Liquid Death which signal workout, not party.
